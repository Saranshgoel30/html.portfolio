Saransh's Favourite Movies Ranked

Top 10 movies

1. Dead Poet Society

The premise of the movie begs you to look at life differently. Outside of stem and conventional journey that you are expected to embark on.

2. The Grave of Fireflies

One of the most heart-wrenching movies I have ever seen. The movie is a reminder of the horrors of war and how it affects the innocent.

3. Spider-Man into the Spiderverse

The best animated movies I have ever seen. The animation is top-notch and the story is heartwarming.

4. Shutter Island

A psychological thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat. The plot twists are mind-boggling and the acting is superb.

5. Top Gun : Maverick

A sequel that surpasses the original. The action sequences are breathtaking and the nostalgia is real.

6. Fight Club

A cult classic that explores the themes of consumerism and identity. The movie is a mind-bender and the ending is iconic.

7. Whiplash

A movie that showcases the relationship between a student and his mentor. The intensity of the movie is unmatched and the performances are stellar.

8. Inside Out

An animated movie that delves into the emotions of a young girl as she navigates a major life change. Creative, insightful, and emotionally resonant.

9. Your Name

An anime movie that transcends cultural boundaries. The story is beautiful and the animation is mesmerizing.

10. Andhadhun

A Hindi movie that is a perfect blend of comedy, thriller, and drama. The plot is unpredictable and the performances are top-notch.
